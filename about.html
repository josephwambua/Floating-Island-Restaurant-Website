Our Founder

Abdullah, founder of Floating Island Restaurant, has transformed the seafood dining scene with his innovative approach to ocean-inspired cuisine. Since opening Floating Island Restaurant, his restaurant has quickly gained acclaim for its fresh, sustainably sourced seafood and inventive dishes, earning numerous awards and a loyal following. Passionate about ocean conservation, Abdullah works with local fishermen and advocates for sustainable fishing practices. Under his leadership, Floating Island Restaurant has become a beacon of culinary excellence and environmental stewardship, setting new standards in the seafood industry.
